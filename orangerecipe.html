Salmon with Fennel, Bell Pepper, and Olives

Orange Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • About

Ingredients

  • 1 navel orange
  • 1 large bulb fennel, cored and thinly sliced, ¼ cup fronds reserved
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 skinless salmon fillets (4 to 6 ounces each)
  • Salt and pepper
  • ⅓ cup pitted black olives, quartered

Instructions

  1. Heat broiler, with rack 8 inches from heat.
  2. Grate 1 tablespoon orange zest, then cut orange into wedges.
  3. On a rimmed baking sheet, toss together orange zest and wedges, fennel, bell pepper, and oil and arrange in an even layer.
  4. Add salmon and season vegetables and salmon with salt and pepper.
  5. Broil until vegetables are browned in spots and salmon is opaque throughout, 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with olives and fennel fronds to serve.

About

Makes 7 servings
