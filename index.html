What is a Rainbow?

A rainbow is a beautiful natural phenomenon consisting of seven distinct colors - red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo and violet - arranged in a colorful arc. Each color has its own unique attributes, which we have turned into distinct flavors and textures.
