Blueberry-Almond Bruschetta

Indigo Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • About

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • Butter, softened
  • Salt and sugar

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice a baguette; arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. Brush with softened butter and sprinkle with salt and sugar.
  3. Bake at 425 degrees F until lightly golden, about 7 minutes. Let cool slightly.
  4. Spread softened butter on the toast rounds.
  5. Top with blueberry preserves and fresh blueberries. Sprinkle with toasted sliced almonds.

About
